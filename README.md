## `> MISSION BRIEFING`

Arctic Howl is a seasonal cybersecurity **Gauntlet** hosted by **Offensive Security (OffSec)**. The narrative follows **Ashka**, an Arctic Wolf and elite cyber defender who was ambushed while investigating anomalous activity in the Tundra data center. Challengers must uncover the truth behind her disappearance while defeating increasingly complex labs.

> Across increasingly difficult labs, competitors must **adapt, learn, and outthink** threats designed to punish stagnation and reward growth.
